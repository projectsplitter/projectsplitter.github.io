How much are your projects
really costing you?

Running multiple Non-Profit or Voluntary Sector projects? Reconciling payroll data with HMRC, Pension companies, Payroll Bureaus? Sick of the endless, error-prone copy and pasting in and out of Excel? Project Splitter takes your Organisations Payroll data and produces detailed reports outlining how funding, including pension and HMRC payments, is being spent.

Project Splitter features

Produce regular monthly reports, on a per Project or per Employee basis, and export them in PDF or CSV format. Keep track of completed months, and use them as a basis for new data (perfect for organisations with static projects and workforce). Or create new monthly calculations from scratch - Project Splitter remembers the projects your organisation works on, the staffing, and the staffing rates and roles.
